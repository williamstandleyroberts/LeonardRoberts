Dr. Leonard E. Roberts

November 24, 1930 - November 24, 2017

This site is devoted to the memory of Dr. Leonard E. Roberts. Late in life Len undertook to commit to paper a life that took him from a small Iowa farmstead, to war, to school and around the globe. His memoirs are preserved here for the edification and enjoyment of friends, family, colleagues and anyone who enjoys a firsthand account of a life well lived.
